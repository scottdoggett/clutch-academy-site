Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-12

Clutch Academy ("we", "our") provides manual transmission driving lessons in Toronto. This page explains what information we collect through our website, how we use it, and which third parties are involved.

What we collect

  • Booking details: When you book a lesson, our scheduling provider Calendly collects your name, email, and the time you select, along with any package preferences and payment information needed to confirm the booking.
  • Analytics: If you grant consent, Google Analytics 4 collects aggregated, de-identified site usage data (pages viewed, approximate location, device type, referral source). IP addresses are anonymized.
  • Advertising measurement: If you grant consent, the Meta Pixel records site visits and interactions (such as clicking "Book Now" or tapping a contact link) so we can measure the performance of our Facebook and Instagram ads and reach similar audiences. We do not upload customer lists to Meta.
  • Direct contact: If you call, email, or message us on social media, we keep that conversation to respond.

How we use it

  • To schedule, confirm, and deliver the driving lesson you booked.
  • To understand which parts of the site visitors find useful so we can improve the experience.
  • To answer questions and respond to inquiries.

We do not sell your personal information. The Meta Pixel is used only to measure ad performance and build audiences within Meta's own platforms (Facebook and Instagram); we do not share customer data with other advertising networks.

Third parties

  • Calendly — handles bookings and payments. See Calendly's privacy policy.
  • Google Analytics 4 — analytics, only after consent. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Meta (Facebook / Instagram) — the Meta Pixel measures ad performance, only after consent. See Meta's privacy policy.
  • Vercel — hosts the website and processes standard server log data (IP address, user agent, request time) for security and reliability.

Cookies

We do not set any analytics or advertising cookies until you click "Accept" on the consent banner. You can change your choice by clearing the clutch.consent.v1 entry from your browser's local storage and reloading.

Your rights

You can request access to or deletion of personal information we hold about you by emailing samuel.anthony@clutchacademy.ca. Ontario residents have additional rights under PIPEDA; we will respond within 30 days.
