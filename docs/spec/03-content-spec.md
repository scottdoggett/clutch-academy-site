## Section 4: Packages & Pricing

**Anchor:** `#packages`

### Headline
Suggested: `Packages & Pricing` or `Simple, Straightforward Pricing`

### Structure — TWO tiers only

✅ **Confirmed: only two pricing tiers. Do not add a third.**

#### Tier 1: Single Lesson
- **Name:** `Single Lesson` (or similar)
- **Price:** `$90` per hour
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Description:** Best for first-timers or refreshers
- **Inclusions (3–5 bullets):** 📎 Pending from client. Example placeholders:
  - One-on-one instruction
  - Clutch control basics
  - First-gear starts and stops
  - Personalized feedback
- **CTA:** `Book This Lesson` — opens Calendly modal

#### Tier 2: 3-Lesson Package ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- **Name:** `3-Lesson Package` (or similar)
- **Price:** `$240` total (equivalent to $80/hr — 11% savings)
- **Duration:** 3 × 1-hour sessions
- **Description:** Best for most learners — full progression from basics to road-confident
- **Inclusions (3–5 bullets):** 📎 Pending from client
- **CTA:** `Book This Package` — opens Calendly modal
- **Visual treatment:** Highlight with a "Most Popular" or "Recommended" badge

### Layout notes
- Side-by-side on desktop, stacked on mobile
- Both cards should be visually similar height; recommended tier uses color/border accent to stand out
- Price should be the largest text on each card

### Subtext below pricing cards
Consider including 1 line about savings on the 3-pack: e.g., "Save $30 with the 3-lesson package" — helps nudge toward the higher-ticket option.

### Payment note
Include a small line near the pricing: `Payment accepted in person — cash, e-transfer, or PayPal.` This sets expectations and avoids booking-vs-payment confusion.

## Section 6: FAQ

**Anchor:** `#faq`

### Headline
Suggested: `Frequently Asked Questions` or `FAQ`

### Structure
Accordion-style (collapsed by default, expand on click). On mobile, accordion is essential for scroll length.

### Questions (9 total)

✅ **Note:** The MTO-certification question from earlier drafts has been REMOVED per client. Do not include it.

Each question below should have an answer populated from the client's copy deck. Placeholder answers are provided where reasonable defaults exist; flag unresolved ones.

1. **Do I need a driver's license to take lessons?**
   - Suggested answer: Yes, a valid G2 or G license is required for all lessons.

2. **I've never touched a stick shift — is that okay?**
   - Suggested answer: Absolutely. Lessons are tailored to your starting skill level, whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refresh.

3. **Where do lessons happen?**
   - 📎 Pending: specific meetup location in Toronto

4. **How many lessons will I need?**
   - Suggested answer: Most students feel confident after 3 lessons. Complete beginners may want the 3-lesson package; drivers with some prior experience often need only 1–2.

5. **What should I wear?**
   - Suggested answer: Thin-soled shoes are best — you need good pedal feel to control the clutch smoothly.

6. **How do I pay?**
   - ✅ Answer: We accept cash, e-transfer, and PayPal. Payment is collected in person at the start of your lesson.

7. **What's the cancellation / reschedule policy?**
   - 📎 Pending from client: exact wording

8. **What car will I learn on?**
   - 📎 Pending from client: make/model and any relevant details

9. **Can I buy a lesson as a gift?**
   - ❓ Open: no gift certificate system is planned. Suggested answer: "We don't currently offer gift certificates, but a friend or family member is welcome to pay for your lesson on your behalf." Confirm with client before publishing.

## Cross-Cutting Content Rules

### Voice and tone
📎 Pending from marketing package. Until defined, default to:
- Warm, direct, jargon-free
- Avoid corporate stiffness
- Avoid overselling — confident, not hyperbolic
- Second person ("you") throughout

### CTAs — canonical labels
Use these labels consistently across the site:

| Context | Label |
|---|---|
| Hero primary CTA | `Start your manual driving journey today` |
| About section CTA | `Book a Lesson with [Name]` |
| Single package CTA | `Book This Lesson` |
| 3-pack CTA | `Book This Package` |
| Final CTA band | `Book Your First Lesson` |
| Nav bar button | `Book Now` |

All route to the same Calendly modal — only the label changes for context.

### Things NOT to write
- ❌ Do not claim MTO certification
- ❌ Do not mention gift certificates (none offered)
- ❌ Do not mention online payment (in-person only)
- ❌ Do not invent the instructor's name, bio, credentials, or car details
- ❌ Do not invent specific testimonial quotes
